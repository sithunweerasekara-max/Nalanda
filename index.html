Nalanda College

Nalanda College, Colombo is a prestigious Buddhist boys’ school established in 1925 and located in the heart of Colombo. Renowned for its academic excellence, discipline, and strong cultural values, the college has built a proud legacy of producing distinguished individuals in fields such as education, leadership, sports, and the arts. With a deep commitment to character building and holistic development, Nalanda provides a vibrant environment where students engage in academics, sports, and extracurricular activities including societies like photography and innovation clubs. Upholding its motto “Character illumines wisdom,” the college continues to guide generations of young Nalandians toward success while preserving its rich heritage and traditions.
